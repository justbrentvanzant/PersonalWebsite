My Portfolio

I make all sorts of games, check out some of my favorites here!

  • Versus

    Versus is a smooth 2D grid based fighting game heavily inspired by Megaman Battle Network. Fight as a variety of fantastical creatures in this player vs player fighting game. Built for UCSD's Triton Gaming Expo, this 2 person fighter is playable on VGDC"s custom built-arcade machine.

  • Jibberish

    Figure out how to blend in a strange society. Solve puzzles involving memorization and positioning. I was the designer behind this game jam entry that won the Overall and Best Design categories of the IDGA San Diego Game Jam. You can play it here.

  • The Climb

    Climb your way to the top in this minimalist arcade game. Control a block trying to make his way up layered platforms that shrink when stepped on. With a time limit of 60 seconds, quick thinking and reflexes are needed to get your block to the top!You can play it here.
